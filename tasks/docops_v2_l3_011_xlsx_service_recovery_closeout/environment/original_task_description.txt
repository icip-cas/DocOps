A field-service operations manager receives a weekly Excel dump with raw ticket rows, customer tiers, SLA policies, part costs, and regional ownership tables. The release process is long and brittle: the raw ticket dump must be normalized, priority must be inferred, owners and SLA targets must be looked up, age and breach flags must be computed from a fixed review timestamp, part exposure must be calculated, exception rows must be separated, customer-facing notice rows must suppress internal notes, dashboard counts must be repaired, raw and scratch sheets must be hidden, and the workbook must be reordered for publication.

This is inspired by common spreadsheet workflow pain points around multi-sheet lookup formulas, conditional formatting, data validation, dashboard repair, and separating internal notes from publishable reports.
